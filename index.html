About the Project

The Jenkins AI Scribe project is designed to implement a cutting-edge AI scribe in the emergency departments of a large health system. Our goal is to streamline documentation processes and improve the quality of patient care by leveraging AI technologies.

Features

  • Real-time scribing for emergency department clinicians
  • Automatic integration with patient medical records
  • Support for multi-language transcription
  • Secure and HIPAA-compliant architecture

Our Team

We are a dedicated group of medical professionals, data scientists, and software engineers collaborating to enhance healthcare delivery using artificial intelligence.
